SUDAR PICHAI CEO OF GOOGLE

  • SUDAR PICHAI CEO OF GOOGLE

Pichai Sundararajan (born June 10, 1972), better known as Sundar Pichai is an Indian-born American business executive. He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. Pichai began his career as a materials engineer. Following a short stint at the management consulting firm McKinsey & Co., Pichai joined Google in 2004, where he led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products, including Google Chrome and ChromeOS, as well as being largely responsible for Google Drive. In addition, he went on to oversee the development of other applications such as Gmail and Google Maps. In 2010, Pichai also announced the open-sourcing of the new video codec VP8 by Google and introduced the new video format, WebM. The Chromebook was released in 2012. In 2013, Pichai added Android to the list of Google products that he oversaw. Pichai was selected to become the next CEO of Google on August 10, 2015, after previously being appointed Product Chief by the then CEO Larry Page. On October 24, 2015, he stepped into the new position at the completion of the formation of Alphabet Inc., the new holding company for the Google company family. He was appointed to the Alphabet Board of Directors in 2017. Pichai was included in Time's annual list of the 100 most influential people in 2016 and 2020.


Early life and education

Pichai was born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India, to a Tamil family. His mother, Lakshmi, was a stenographer, and his father, Regunatha Pichai, was an electrical engineer at GEC, the British conglomerate. Pichai completed schooling in Jawahar Vidyalaya Senior Secondary School in Ashok Nagar, Chennai and completed the Class XII from Vana Vani school at IIT Madras. He earned his degree from IIT Kharagpur in metallurgical engineering and is a distinguished alumnus from that institution.He holds an M.S. from Stanford University in materials science and engineering, and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania, where he was named a Siebel Scholar and a Palmer Scholar, respectively.


LIFE STORY OF SUDAR PICHAI


  1. Venturing to the United States: After completing his undergraduate studies, Pichai moved to the United States to pursue further education. He obtained a Master of Science degree in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University and later earned an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
  2. Career at Google: Pichai joined Google in 2004, initially working on the Google Toolbar and other software products. His talent and dedication quickly caught the attention of Google's co-founders, Larry Page and Sergey Brin. Pichai played a crucial role in the development of various Google products, including Google Chrome, which became one of the most popular web browsers globally.
  3. Leadership Roles: Pichai continued to rise through the ranks at Google, assuming leadership roles in several key projects. In 2013, he became the Senior Vice President of Android, overseeing the development of Google's mobile operating system. Under his leadership, Android became the most widely used mobile operating system in the world.
  4. CEO of Google: In August 2015, Sundar Pichai was appointed as the CEO of Google, following the restructuring of the company into Alphabet Inc. He became responsible for overseeing Google's core business operations, including search, advertising, YouTube, and Android, among others.
  5. Expansion of Alphabet Inc.: As CEO of Google, Pichai played a pivotal role in expanding the company's reach and diversifying its portfolio. Under his leadership, Google made significant advancements in artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and hardware products.
  6. Transformation of Google: Pichai's tenure as CEO has been marked by significant milestones, including the integration of artificial intelligence across Google's products and services, the expansion of the company's cloud computing business, and the continued innovation in areas such as self-driving cars and healthcare.

    7. Sundar Pichai's journey exemplifies the power of hard work, perseverance, and innovation in achieving success. His leadership has not only propelled Google to new heights but has also cemented his position as one of the most influential tech executives of his generation.
